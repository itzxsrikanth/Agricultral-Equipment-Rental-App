Frequently Asked Questions

Clear up common doubts about booking terms, logistics, security deposits, and machinery maintenance.

To protect both owners and renters, we require simple identity verification (Aadhaar, driver's license, or land record ID) along with phone number verification before any booking is approved.

During checkout, you can choose "Self-Pickup" to collect the machine from the owner's farm, or request "Owner Delivery" for a small mileage-based surcharge. You can coordinate scheduling details directly via the booking chat.

If a technical failure occurs due to normal wear and tear, owners are responsible for repair costs and providing a replacement. However, if damage occurs due to operator negligence or misuse, repairs are funded from the renter's deposit/insurance.

Simply click the "Register" button in the navigation bar and select the role "Owner". Once registered, you will unlock a dedicated dashboard allowing you to add, manage, edit, and track your agricultural assets.
